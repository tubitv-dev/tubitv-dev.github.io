What is Tubi TV?

Tubi TV is a free, ad-supported streaming service that offers a massive library of movies, TV shows, live news, and sports. Owned by Fox Corporation, Tubi has quickly become one of the most popular free streaming platforms in the United States, Canada, Mexico, and Australia. With thousands of titles spanning every genre — from action and comedy to drama, horror, and documentaries — Tubi provides endless entertainment without requiring a subscription fee.

Unlike many streaming giants, Tubi is 100% free. You don't need a credit card, and there are no hidden charges. All you need is an internet connection and a device to stream. However, to enjoy personalized recommendations, resume watching across devices, and save your favorite shows, you'll want to create a free Tubi TV account and log in.

Why create a Tubi TV account?

While Tubi allows you to browse and watch content without an account, logging in unlocks several valuable features that enhance your streaming experience:

  • 📌 Resume watching — Pick up right where you left off on any device.
  • ❤️ Save favorites — Build a watchlist of movies and shows you love.
  • 🎯 Personalized recommendations — Get suggestions based on what you watch.
  • 🔄 Sync across devices — Start on your phone, continue on your TV.
  • 🔒 Parental controls — Restrict content for younger viewers.

Creating an account takes less than a minute — you just need an email address and a password. Once you log in, all these features are available instantly.

💡
Pro Tip: Use the same email and password across all devices. This ensures your watch history and preferences sync seamlessly, so you never lose track of what you're watching.

How to log in to Tubi TV on the web (Desktop / Laptop)

Logging in to Tubi TV from your computer is quick and straightforward. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Open your preferred web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge) and go to tubitv.com.
Step 2: In the top-right corner of the homepage, click the "Log In" button.
Step 3: Enter the email address associated with your Tubi account.
Step 4: Type your password. (Make sure Caps Lock is off and you're using the correct case.)
Step 5: Click the "Log In" button. You'll be redirected to the Tubi homepage, and you'll see your profile name or avatar in the top-right corner — confirming you're logged in.
⚠️
Security Note: Always log out when using a public or shared computer. Click your profile icon and select "Log Out" to protect your account.

How to log in to Tubi TV on mobile (Android & iOS)

The Tubi mobile app is available on both the Google Play Store and Apple App Store. Here's how to log in:

Step 1: Download and install the official Tubi TV app from the Play Store (Android) or App Store (iOS).
Step 2: Open the app. On the welcome screen, tap "Log In" (usually at the bottom or top-right).
Step 3: Enter your registered email address and password.
Step 4: Tap "Log In". You'll be taken to the Tubi home screen, where you can start browsing or continue watching.
📱
Mobile Tip: Enable "Stay Logged In" if you're using your personal device to avoid re-entering your credentials every time. You can manage this in the app's settings.

How to log in to Tubi TV on Smart TVs & Streaming Devices

Tubi is available on a wide range of smart TVs and streaming devices, including Roku, Amazon Fire TV, Apple TV, Android TV, Samsung Smart TV, LG Smart TV, and more. The login process is slightly different:

Step 1: Launch the Tubi app on your device (download it from the device's app store if you haven't already).
Step 2: On the welcome screen, select "Log In".
Step 3: You'll see an activation code displayed on your TV screen (a 6-character alphanumeric code).
Step 4: On your phone or computer, go to tubitv.com/activate and enter the code.
Step 5: Log in with your Tubi credentials on the activation page. Your TV will automatically refresh and you'll be logged in.
🔄
Activation expires: The activation code is temporary. If it expires, simply generate a new code by selecting "Log In" again on your TV.

Forgot your Tubi TV password? Reset it

If you've forgotten your password, don't worry — resetting it takes just a couple of minutes:

Step 1: Go to the Tubi login page (on the web or in the app).
Step 2: Click or tap the "Forgot Password?" link below the login fields.
Step 3: Enter the email address you used to sign up.
Step 4: Check your inbox for a password reset email from Tubi. (If you don't see it, check your spam or junk folder.)
Step 5: Click the link in the email and follow the instructions to create a new password.
Step 6: Return to the login page and sign in with your new password.
🔐
Password security: Choose a strong, unique password that you don't use for other accounts. A mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols is recommended.

Troubleshooting — Common login issues & fixes

Sometimes you might encounter issues while trying to log in. Here are the most common problems and how to resolve them:

❌ Incorrect email or password

Double-check that you're using the correct email address and password. Passwords are case-sensitive, so ensure Caps Lock is off. If you're unsure, use the "Forgot Password?" feature to reset it.

❌ Account not verified

When you first create a Tubi account, you receive a verification email. If you haven't verified your email, you won't be able to log in. Check your inbox (and spam folder) for the verification link and click it to activate your account.

❌ Browser cache / cookies issue

Sometimes outdated cache or cookies can interfere with the login process. Clear your browser's cache and cookies, then try again. You can also try using incognito/private mode.

❌ App version outdated

If you're using the mobile app and experiencing login issues, check the app store for updates. An outdated app version may have compatibility issues.

❌ Regional restrictions

Tubi is currently available in the United States, Canada, Mexico, and Australia. If you're outside these regions, you may not be able to log in or stream content. Use a VPN if you're traveling, but note that Tubi's terms of service may restrict VPN usage.

🛠️
Still stuck? Visit the Tubi Help Center at help.tubitv.com for additional support, or contact Tubi's customer service team directly.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

🔹 Do I need a Tubi TV account to watch?

No, you can browse and watch content without an account. However, you won't have access to features like resume watching, personalized recommendations, and watchlists. An account is free and takes less than a minute to create.

🔹 Is Tubi TV really free?

Yes! Tubi is 100% free and supported by ads. You never need to enter a credit card or pay a subscription fee. The ads are short and help keep the service free for everyone.

🔹 Can I log in to Tubi on multiple devices?

Absolutely. You can log in to your Tubi account on as many devices as you want — phone, tablet, computer, TV, and more. Your watch history and preferences will sync across all devices.

🔹 How do I log out of Tubi TV?

On the web, click your profile icon in the top-right corner and select "Log Out." On mobile and TV apps, go to Settings or your profile, and choose "Log Out" or "Sign Out."

🔹 What should I do if I don't receive the password reset email?

First, check your spam or junk folder. If you still don't see it, wait a few minutes and try again. Make sure you're entering the correct email address. If the issue persists, contact Tubi support.

🔹 Can I delete my Tubi account?

Yes. You can request account deletion by contacting Tubi customer support. Keep in mind that deleting your account will remove your watch history, favorites, and preferences permanently.
